ImgurGeeks Options

About

This extension is a labor of love for Imgur. A bunch of us power-users wanted a "scoreboard" showing data about our posts and images views. This extension is the result.

Got a bug/suggestion? Please check out the forum.


Misc experiments

Show an extra button on the imgur post page to do queue up a post for a later time.

Note: Settings are automatically saved
If you're having issues or just want to clear all data, hold down the shift key while chosing Actions->Remove... in the main extension page.
